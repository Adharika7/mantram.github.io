Mantram Healthcare Service

Founded by Dr. Divisha Agrawal, our clinic is dedicated to improving the health and quality of life of patients across all age groups. We focus on restoring strength, reducing pain, and rebuilding confidence through specialised physiotherapy care.

Mantram Healthcare Service is a trusted physiotherapy clinic located at 61, Jal Vihar Colony, Raipur, Chhattisgarh 492001. Over the years, our centre has grown into a place where modern physiotherapy meets compassionate patient care. From children with developmental needs to adults recovering from sports injuries and elderly patients living with chronic conditions, we ensure every patient receives personalised treatment.

Our Approach

We believe no two patients are alike. Every case starts with a detailed assessment, followed by a tailored treatment plan. By combining evidence-based physiotherapy techniques with personal care, we aim to help patients recover and prevent future issues.

We emphasise patient education, teaching posture correction, ergonomics, and simple home exercises to extend recovery beyond the clinic. We also use digital tools so patients and families can track progress effectively.
